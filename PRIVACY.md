# Privacy Policy: Web Visiting Breaker Management Console

**Effective Date:** June 5, 2026

## 1. Overview
The **Web Visiting Breaker Management Console** is designed with a "privacy-first" philosophy. Our primary mission is to provide tools for regulated web access—enabling safety and productivity—while keeping user data under the user's control.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
To fulfill its single purpose of regulating web access, the extension processes the following data:

### Web History (URLs)
*   **Local Enforcement:** The extension monitors the URLs of websites you visit to compare them against your configured block/allow lists and enforcement policies.
*   **Local Logging:** If enabled, browsing history and focus-time data are stored exclusively within your browser's local IndexedDB. This data is used only to provide you with history reports and productivity insights within the Management Console.
*   **Real-Time Categorization (Autopilot):** If the "Autopilot" feature is enabled, the extension may send the host portion of an unclassified URL to your self-configured or designated Autopilot server. This request is used solely to determine the website's category (e.g., "Social Media", "Education") and does not include personally identifiable information.

## 3. Data Storage and Protection
*   **Local-Only Storage:** Your browsing history, logs, and configuration settings are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any cloud-based database of your browsing activity.
*   **No Data Selling:** We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.
*   **No Advertising:** Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, or creditworthiness assessments.
*   **Encryption:** Sensitive configuration data and local logs are managed within the extension's secure storage.

## 4. User Control and Transparency
*   **Log Management:** You can view, manage, and clear your local browsing logs at any time through the Management Console.
*   **Policy Control:** You have full control over which categories and URLs are regulated.
*   **Opt-Out:** Disabling or uninstalling the extension immediately ceases all data processing.

## 5. Security
The Management Console includes an optional password protection feature to prevent unauthorized local access to your configuration and browsing logs.

## 6. Contact and Updates
We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us through the official support channels provided on the Chrome Web Store.
